Navigating Visa Processes with Confidence

Securing a student visa is a critical step in your international education journey. At Tundua Edu Consult, we provide comprehensive guidance through complex visa application procedures, helping you avoid common pitfalls while maintaining our impressive 95% visa success rate.

Our Visa Support Services

Our structured approach to visa applications ensures thorough preparation and documentation across all major study destinations:

Country-Specific Requirements Analysis

We provide detailed breakdowns of the latest visa requirements for your destination country, including financial thresholds, health insurance needs, language proficiency evidence, and specific documentation formats.

Documentation Preparation

Our visa specialists guide you through preparing and organizing all required documents, including financial statements, academic transcripts, standardized test scores, proof of enrollment, statement of purpose, accommodation arrangements, and health certificates.

Application Review and Submission

We conduct thorough reviews of all application materials before submission, checking for completeness, accuracy, and consistency to minimize the risk of delays or rejections.

Interview Preparation

For countries requiring visa interviews, we provide comprehensive preparation through mock interview sessions, guidance on commonly asked questions, advice on professional presentation, and strategies for addressing potential concerns.

Destination-Specific Expertise

While visa requirements vary significantly between countries, our team maintains up-to-date expertise on processes for all major study destinations:

Destination Key Visa Features
United Kingdom Student visa routes, financial requirements, CAS validation
United States F-1 visa applications, SEVIS registration, I-20 processing
Canada Study permit applications, biometrics requirements, GIC details
Australia Student visa subclasses, genuine temporary entrant criteria
Europe Schengen and non-Schengen processes, residence permits
New Zealand Fee assessment, medical requirements, pathway options
